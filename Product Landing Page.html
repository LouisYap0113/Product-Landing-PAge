Open Language

About Us

Open Language is a conversational English-learning APP created by professional foreign teachers.

The English learning system here is designed specifically for Asians by teaching and research experts from Columbia University and experienced native English teachers from North America.

Within this App, you can listen to spoken English lessons that cover a large variety of topics such as daily life, workplace, and going aboard.

We always insist: When learning English, we should be able to understand and speak.
